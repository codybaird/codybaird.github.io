ADmen - Founder. Contributor at Google, LocalU, and Local Search Forum. Speaker at LocalU.

Involved with all things "search" since 2005. In 2011, I founded ADmen.com, a firm specializing in search marketing brand strategy.

My vision is to shine a light on local greatness; The Driskill Hotel, Tacodeli, or Franklin BBQ. I cringe to think of a world with Applebee's but no Franklin BBQ, Hyatt's but no Driskill Hotel.

I created ADmen out of respect for originality, American craftmenship, and the entrepreneurial spirit. Locals love their neighbors, hometown and do what is in the best interest of the community.

Specialties and focus: search marketing and optimization (SEO), local search (SEO), responsive web design, GEO marketing, PPC, display advertising, local brand development and messaging.

To relax or blow off steam I read on a variety of subjects, crossfit or trail running daily, golfing, hiking, snowboarding, or anything outdoors.
